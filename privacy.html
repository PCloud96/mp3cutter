Privacy Policy

MP3 Cutter: Ringtone & Trim

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

MP3 Cutter ("the App") is developed by Cloud Motion Lab. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. The App processes audio files entirely on your device. No audio files, recordings, or personal information are uploaded to any server.

Audio Files

All audio processing (trimming, gain adjustment, format conversion) happens locally on your iPhone. Your audio files never leave your device. We cannot access, view, or store your audio content.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and accessible through your Apple ID settings.

Analytics

The App may use Apple's built-in analytics (App Analytics) to understand general usage patterns such as app opens and feature usage. This data is anonymized and aggregated by Apple. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not share data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require an account or any personal information to function.

Data Storage

App preferences (such as export quality settings) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. No data is synced to external servers.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your audio files and app data can be removed by deleting the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
